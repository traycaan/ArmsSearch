Note on Sources:
- Sources with * are included in the search tabs
- I am not responsible for the content on any of these sites
- To suggest sites go the the about page

7.62x54r.net

A trove of information pertaining to Mosin Nagant rifles and the 7.62x54r caliber. Go to

American Society of Arms Collectors*

Founded in 1953 for the research, collection and preservation of firearms the American Society of Arms Collectors has many of its members' articles published on their website.

Artillery Luger

Written by Mauro Baudino, an accomplished firearms historian and webmaster, this site contains many useful articles about the Artillery Luger variants.

Bavarian M1 Carbines

Created to fill a hole left by researchers about M1 Carbines in Germany and Austria after WWII Bavarian M1 Carbines has articles pertaining to the use of the weapons by the German and Austrian police forces.

Bayonets of Scandinavia

Articles on the many bayonets of the Scandinavian countries.

Carbines For Collectors*

Covering the arms used by the nations during the era of the world wars, Carbines for Collectors lays out the history of small arms for each nation.

Chinese SKS Guide

As their mission statement reads, they are dedicated to the study, evolution and theory of the Chinese SKS. The site has many articles on a large varitey of SKS types.

eBayonet*

Dennis Ottobre's site sells and collects bayonets while also providing researched articles of these bayonets and small arms.

Forgotten Weapons*

Companion site to the Forgotten Weapons Youtube channel by Ian McCollum, ForgottenWeapons.com is a repository of firearms information and regularly updated blog.

Gothia Arms

Created by Olof Janson, this site has many small arms articles, mainly focusing on Swedish firearms.

HARC-MCRRS

Historic Arms Resource Centre - Miniature Calibre Rifles Research Site. Articles on British and Commonwealth military and target rifles, etc.

m9130.info

Soviet firearms history. Documents the long history soviet firearms and their design.

MAC-11.info

Created to centralize information around the M-11 family of firearms and to dispell the myths and bad information surrounding the weapon.

Manowar's Hungarian Weapons & History

An exhaustive list of information about firearms thoughtout Hungary's history.

Modern Firearms*

Maintained and written by Maxim Popenker containing over 1,200 articles on historical and modern firearms.

MP44 Collectors Service

A service for German semi-automatic WWII rifle owners, the site has a lot of infomation pertaining to these rifles and their care.

Municion*

Created by the Spanish Association of Cartridge Collectors, Municion maintains a database of cartridge information.

Old Colt

Anything and everything Colt in this exhaustive repository.

Old Military Markings

German, Japanese, British and Swiss rifle markings.

Paul Mauser Archive

Written by Mauro Baudino, an accomplished firearms historian and webmaster, this site contains many useful articles about Paul Mauser and his firearms.

Pistolen der Deutschen Wehrmacht

A repository of the many pistols of the German Wehrmacht, as well as many other counries during that time.

Royal Armouries*

The Royal Armouries is one of the foremost arms and armour museums in the world. The online collections have many of it's most interesting artifacts displayed.

Spanish Civil War Mosin

In great detail the history and study of Mosin Nagant rifles in the Spanish Civil War is documented.

Star Firearms

A website dedicated to the weapons made by the Spanish firearms Manufactuer Star Bonifacio Echeverria.

Surplused*

Made by C&R's Othais as a place for firearms collectors to aggragate articles and videos.

Swiss Rifles

Dedicated to the unique history of magazine-fed cartridge rifles in Switzerland.

The History of French Rifles

Created by Steve Jackson about mostly untold history and development of the French rifle.

The Rifle Sling*

Created to fill a hole in the firearms community the site documents rifle slings from around the world.

Thinline Weapons*

Archive of dead and dying websites and Soviet arms wiki.

US Rifle Cal 30 M1

A variety of information regarding the M1 Garand rifle including production dates and a database of Garands.

Unblinking Eye*

Written by Ed Buffaloe, this website documents his many interests including his gun collecting. it contains a good many researched articles on select firearms.

Vestpocket Pistol Collector

Technical articles on a multitude of european pocket pistols.

World Bayonets*

Over 5,000 pages on the world's bayonets.
