Welcome to Arms-Search.com

The goal of this site is to provide an easy way to research firearm's related questions without the fluff a normal Google search. Because of this the search function only searches from a curated list of websites with good information (see Sources).

The idea for this site came to me after watching a video by Ian McCullum (here) explaining why the internet has replaced museums for information about unusual and rare firearms.

"Tips and Tricks" for using the search function are located on the Instructions page. If you have any suggestions for the site or sources to include use the box below.
